About The SLN DEGREE COLLEGE

S.L.N. Degree college is a premier educational institution in Anantapur. Smt J Jayalakshmi, the founder head, started the institution in 2016 with a noble aim of dispensing quality higher education at a low cost to the rural women. The college is set in picturesque surroundings in a spacious campus in Alamur road, Anantapur. Just in a span of three years, the college carved a niche for itself as the best women's college in Anantapur. The credit of it goes entirely to the founder correspondent, Smt J Jayalakshmi for the meticulous implementation of his noble vision. The management firmly believes that education of women lifts families, communities and countries out of poverty. Hence, he and his college have left no stone unturned to provide the best infrastructure, the best teachers and the best ambience to its wards. The students have been making the best use of the facilities in achieving academic excellence. Besides education, the college strives for the wholesome development of its students involving them in various activities that boost their confidence and competence required to thrive in any situation.
